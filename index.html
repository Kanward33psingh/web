WHY CHOSE US

Welcome to Crawford Farm Park, where nature meets fun! Located in the heart of the countryside, our family-owned farm park offers a delightful escape from the hustle and bustle of city life. Step into a world of lush green fields, friendly farm animals, and exciting activities for all ages. Whether you're looking to enjoy a leisurely stroll, pet adorable animals, or embark on an educational adventure, Crawford Farm Park has something for everyone. Immerse yourself in the beauty of nature, create cherished memories, and experience the joy of rural life. Come and discover the magic of Crawford Farm Park today!

SHOP

DESCRIPTION

At Crawford Farm Park, our shop is a hidden gem waiting to be explored. Step inside and be greeted by a delightful array of artisanal products and locally sourced treasures. From handmade crafts to farm-fresh produce, our shop offers a unique and diverse range of goods. Browse through shelves filled with organic jams, aromatic candles, handcrafted pottery, and more. Warm smiles and friendly conversations await as our knowledgeable staff guide you through the offerings, sharing stories behind the items and providing recommendations. Savor the aroma of freshly brewed coffee as you take a moment to relax in our cozy café area, where you can indulge in homemade treats made with love. Discover the joy of supporting local artisans and savoring the flavors of the region at the Crawford Farm Park shop, where every visit is an opportunity to connect with the community and take a piece of the countryside home with you.

ZOO

Welcome to the Crawford Farm Park Zoo, where a world of wonder and wildlife awaits. Explore our diverse collection of animals from around the globe, from majestic lions to playful monkeys and graceful birds. Immerse yourself in interactive exhibits and educational presentations that bring you closer to the fascinating creatures that call our zoo home. Discover the magic of the animal kingdom and embark on an unforgettable adventure at the Crawford Farm Park Zoo.

At the Crawford Farm Park Zoo, we're passionate about conservation and providing a safe haven for our animal residents. Learn about their habitats, behaviors, and the importance of preserving their natural environments. From endangered species to local wildlife, our zoo showcases the beauty and diversity of the animal kingdom, inspiring visitors of all ages to appreciate and protect our precious planet. Come and witness the wonders of nature up close at the Crawford Farm Park Zoo.
